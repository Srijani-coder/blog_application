GOOGLE SITEMAP + SEO SETUP FOR JUICYSTATCONTROVERSY

What was added:
1. /sitemap.xml
   Dynamic sitemap listing:
   - homepage
   - /posts
   - every blog article /post/<slug>

2. /robots.txt
   Allows Google to crawl public pages and points Google to the sitemap.
   Admin and unsubscribe pages are blocked from crawling.

3. SEO metadata in base.html
   - meta description
   - meta keywords
   - canonical URL
   - robots index/follow
   - OpenGraph metadata
   - Twitter card metadata

4. BlogPosting JSON-LD in post.html
   Each article now exposes structured data:
   - headline
   - author
   - published date
   - modified date
   - contextual keywords
   - image if available

After deployment, open these URLs:
https://statdash.onrender.com/sitemap.xml
https://statdash.onrender.com/robots.txt

Submit to Google:
1. Go to Google Search Console.
2. Select your property: https://statdash.onrender.com
3. Go to Indexing > Sitemaps.
4. Enter: sitemap.xml
5. Click Submit.

For each important post:
1. Go to URL Inspection.
2. Paste the article URL, for example:
   https://statdash.onrender.com/post/your-post-slug
3. Click Request indexing.

Important note:
Google does not rank pages just because keywords are added to a sitemap. Sitemaps help discovery. Ranking depends on article quality, titles, headings, internal links, backlinks, performance, mobile usability, and matching search intent. The code adds contextual metadata and schema so Google can better understand your article topics.
